How to Play

Journey from early apes through key hominin species to modern humans. Manage food, health, morale, and distance. Travel to progress, rest to recover, forage/hunt for food, and innovate to unlock advances. Random events will help or hinder you. Reach Homo sapiens with your group alive to win.

  • Travel advances distance but costs food and morale.
  • Rest restores health and morale but consumes time and some food.
  • Forage/Hunt gain food; success varies by species and era.
  • Innovate unlocks tools and cognition; evolve at milestones.
